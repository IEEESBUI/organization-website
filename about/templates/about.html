About IEEE SBUI

Learn about our history, mission, and the team behind IEEE Student Branch Universitas Indonesia

Who We Are

IEEE Student Branch Universitas Indonesia is the first IEEE Student Branch in Indonesia. In the past years, we have organized programs for the developments of DTE UI students, such as: Webinar Series, Workshop, IEEE xTreme Selection, World Telecommunication Day with Telco Lab UI, IEEE Volunteers, and so much more.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

1. Cultivate a sense of belonging and strengthen internal bonds to create a supportive and unified community, driving effective teamwork, collaboration, and shared enthusiasm in achieving organizational goals.
2. Develop an organization that prioritizes advancement through professional contributions and collaborative innovation, empowering members to grow both technically and professionally while promoting a culture of constant progress.
3. Enhance visibility and professional engagement, by actively showcasing its initiatives and establishing a presence that inspires and attracts a diverse and passionate community.
4. Amplify involvement of members and internal alike, nurturing inclusive participation and ensuring the organization's vision resonates with a broader audience.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of innovation, empowering our members to lead and inspire in the field of electrical and electronics engineering, sparking the future with brilliance and ingenuity.

Our Core Values

Unity

This value emphasizes the power of working together, fostering a sense of belonging and mutual support across all divisions. It’s about creating stronger connections, improving communication, and ensuring that everyone collaborates effectively toward shared goals. When members feel united, the whole organization thrives through collective effort and shared enthusiasm.

Empowerment

Empowerment is about providing the tools, resources, and opportunities for personal and professional growth. It encourages individuals to take initiative, develop new skills, and feel confident in their abilities. By empowering members with knowledge, experiences, and leadership opportunities, IEEE SBUI can cultivate a culture of innovation and self-driven progress.

Impact

Impact focuses on making a transformative difference, both within the organization and in the broader community. Whether through visibility, engagement, or contributing to technological advancements, this value highlights the importance of meaningful actions. By maximizing the impact of its activities, IEEE SBUI can inspire others, influence change, and leave a lasting legacy in the field of technology and innovation.
